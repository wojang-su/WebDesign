What is ADHD?

In 1798, Six Alex Crichton was first person who described ADHD as "A mental restlessness". In 1902, Sir George Still described ADHD again "An abnormal defcect in moral control in children".

ADHD, Attention Deficit hyperactivity disorder is a condition, which affects parts of brains that control attention, impulses and concentration.
