Diet Therapy

What is Diet Therapy?

The diet therapy is a diet treatment for ADHD. The ADHD diet optimizes brain functions and could have beneficial effects on the ADHD symptoms.

Dr. Josh. Axe introduces good foods, supplement, and natural treatments for ADHD.
First, patients have to avoid sugar and gluten. Sugar is a main problem that causes blood-sugar spikes. When blood-sugar spikes go down, people start to lose their focus ability. If blood-sugar spikes go up, then people tend to show hyperactive behaviors.

Second, consuming healthy protein, fat, and fiber would help to steady blood-sugar. Examples for healthful food are vegetable, fruit, nuts, seeds, and organic meats.

Third, omega-3 fatty acids is important to improve the ADHD symptoms. For instance, fish oil and cod liver oil.

Fourth, Vitamin B complex help to make the ADHD symptoms better. Vitamin B complex contains vitamin B6, vitamin B12, folate and biotin. Vitamin B complex also supports nervous system and brain to focus well.
Axe J.,(2015).
