Opinion about Treatments

“It’s more like my eyeglasses.I have a problem with my eyes. I can’t read typewriter-size print without my glasses. If I have them on, I can read it about as well as anybody can, but if I take them off, it just looks blurry to me and I can’t see. Just as my eyglasses, medications do not help me when the medicine is worn off."
Brown T.,(2016).

  • Click to watch the video

    • In my opinion, as like an "eyeglasses", medication does not help to cure the symptom, but it helps to control the symptoms. I believe the medication does not cause serious side effect as like depression. Many negative side effects of medications for ADHD in the Internet websites is over exaggerated.

    All the treatments works differently for different patients. Individual factors should be considered before selecting the treatments for ADHD patients. I recommend to consult with a doctor before trying a new treatment.
